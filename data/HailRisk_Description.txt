Description of variables:


evt_yr - calendar year of hail event
HailSize - size of hail
BLDG_AGE - age of the property
BLDG_LMT - amount of coverage
deductible - deductible 
ShingleRoof - 1 indicates shingle roof and 0 otherwise
FrameConstruction - 1 indicates frame construction and 0 otherwise
SingleFamily - 1 indicates single family and 0 otherwise
RPT_Delay - reporting delay from hail occurrence to the time of reporting
paidloss - amount of paid losses
